About

Sarah is a Management Consultant providing support to the NHS, having worked in the NHS in primary care for 25 years, both as a commissioner and as a front-line provider.

She began her career as a Practice and Fundholding Manager in a first wave Fundholding Practice. In 1999, as Fundholding came to an end, she moved into commissioning, with responsibility for developing and commissioning primary, community and mental health services. She remained in that role until 2004, when she made the decision to move back to the coal face and returned to Practice Management in a 14,000-patient, forward-thinking and progressive first wave PMS Plus Practice.

During Sarah’s 12 years at the practice, the town was one of the Department of Health’s Integrated Care Organisation Pilot sites. As part of this, she had the opportunity to lead work in a truly integrated way across primary and community care and see the positive impact that this had on patient care and outcomes, as well as the better and more efficient use of scarce resource.

Sarah has also been a Director of a GP Federation, and through that role played a key part in developing the successful application to become one of 15 Rapid Test Sites for Primary Care Home, which is a multi-speciality community provider model of care.

Sarah has always enjoyed participating in and delivering training. She worked with the then Northern Deanery to write and deliver a training manual to Practice Managers working in GP Training Practices. She has also had a regular slot on the local GP Vocational Training Scheme, where she had the opportunity to impart her wisdom on the business side of general practice to GP Registrars.

Sarah is innovative, financially astute and has excellent interpersonal skills. She understands the importance of getting to know people and what motivates them and this then helps her to get the best out of those she works with; managing change and resolving conflict and challenge. She is passionate about primary care and its importance in delivering high quality care and outcomes to patients.

Since becoming a management consultant Sarah has continued to work at the sharp end of primary care service delivery, doing such as supporting practices to work at scale and develop back office function, primary care project assurance work, and delivering turn around and change management to practices struggling with the demands placed on them.

Services

Workforce Development

Is the shape of your workforce not as you want or need it to be in order to deliver your desired outcomes? Are you experiencing difficulties within your workforce? I can support you to identify the changes that need to be made, or the challenges that need to be resolved, and then work with you and your team to deliver the necessary changes and bring resolution to challenges or conflict.

Practice Turnaround

If your practice is experiencing difficulties, whatever they may be and for whatever reason, I have the skills and experience to provide objective analysis of your current position and then work with your practice team to establish the changes needed to bring about turn around and then support you to deliver the changes needed to make your practice stronger and more resilient, with improved patient and financial outcomes.

Coaching

Do you have new or existing members of staff who need support to develop their skills and realize their potential? I am able to work one to one, or with teams, to develop confidence, provide training and motivation and ensure that skills are maximised and your team works as efficiently and effectively as possible.

Development of Back Office Function

Whether formally merging with other practices, forming alliances or taking other collaborative approaches, I am able to support the development of a combined back office function. This comes both from the point of view of ensuring staff engage proactively in the process as well as developing and establishing formal systems and processes to ensure that the back office functions effectively with a system of governance, responsibility and accountability.

Project Management and Assurance Work

Whether you have a specific project to deliver within primary care, or as commissioners you require assurance that a project is being delivered within the timescales and funding agreed, we have the skills, expertise and a toolkit that we have developed to support the process.
